What is Neema Global Foundation (NGF)? Who we are
▾

Neema Global Foundation (NGF) is a mission-driven foundation advancing sustainable social justice by empowering women, widows, and youth through economic empowerment, skills development, inclusive partnerships, and climate resilience—across Kenya.

2
What is your vision and mission? Vision • Mission
▾

Mission: Empower women, widows, and youth to overcome poverty through economic empowerment, decent work opportunities, and pathways to dignified, self-reliant livelihoods.

Slogan: HOPE AND MERCY IN ACTION

3
Where do you work in Kenya? Counties reached
▾

NGF currently operates through community-led programs and partnerships in multiple counties. On the website you’ll see our active reach highlighted in:

  • Kitui
  • Nakuru
  • Kiambu
  • Nyeri

As partnerships expand, our reach scales without compromising dignity, quality, and accountability.

4
What programs do you run? What we do
▾

Our programs are structured around six core objectives:

  • Poverty Reduction & Decent Work
  • Skills Development & Youth Leadership
  • Economic Empowerment & Climate-Smart Skills
  • Food Security & Agribusiness Development
  • Community Empowerment & Social Justice
  • Climate Action & Environmental Resilience

See full detail on the Programs page.

5
Who can benefit from NGF programs? Eligibility
▾

Our primary focus is on widows, women, and youth—especially those facing economic exclusion, limited access to skills and decent work, or vulnerability to climate and market shocks.

Beneficiary selection is done in partnership with community structures to ensure fairness, dignity, and protection for vulnerable households.

6
How can I donate to NGF? Payment methods
▾

You can donate through M-PESA, bank transfer, or card options as presented on the Donate page.

Tip: Use the Donate page for the official paybill/bank details and the latest giving instructions (kept consistent across all pages).

7
What does my donation actually do? Impact per amount
▾

Donations directly support dignified livelihoods—training, tools, coaching, and resilience-building. On the Donate page you’ll see clear examples such as:

  • KES 2,000 — supports essential household relief for a family (short-term stability)
  • KES 5,000 — contributes to skills training materials / starter tools
  • KES 10,000+ — supports enterprise coaching, group savings support, or climate-smart inputs

We avoid exaggeration—impact is presented transparently and updated as programs scale.

8
How can I get involved besides donating? Volunteer • Partner • Sponsor
▾

NGF offers clear engagement paths:

  • Volunteer your time or skills
  • Partner with us (NGOs, churches, institutions, and community groups)
  • Sponsor a widow (dignified, structured support)
  • Corporate sponsorship and CSR partnerships

Start on Get Involved or reach out via Contact.

9
Do you accept partnerships and funding opportunities? Partners
▾

Yes. NGF invites donors and partners to collaborate in scaling high-impact, community-led solutions that unlock the potential of widows, women, and youth while strengthening climate resilience and inclusive growth.

We welcome co-designed partnerships with clear KPIs and transparent reporting.

10
How do you ensure accountability and transparency? Governance • Reporting
▾

NGF applies evidence-based programming with measurable indicators, baseline/endline assessments, and adaptive learning to inform scale and sustainability.

You’ll find assessment documents and reports in the Media page (PDF uploads) and key highlights in Impact.

11
Is it safe to donate online? What about my data? Security • Privacy
▾

We use a trust-first design approach: minimal data collection, clear payment instructions, and privacy-aware contact forms.

  • We only request what we need (e.g., name/email if you choose to share).
  • We don’t sell donor data or share it for advertising.
  • Payment pages emphasize verified details and fraud-awareness tips.

If anything looks suspicious, use the Contact page to verify official channels.

12
Where can I watch your documentaries and press mentions? Media
▾

Visit the Media page for featured documentaries (short + long), press mentions (YouTube videos), and downloadable assessment reports (PDF).

13
How do I contact NGF? Reach us
▾

Use the Contact page for official channels (email, phone, location, and partnership inquiries).
