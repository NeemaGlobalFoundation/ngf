Why it matters

Widows in Kenya remain among the most structurally vulnerable populations—facing economic exclusion, social stigma, and reduced access to protection systems. NGF responds with coordinated solutions that integrate livelihoods, skills, and resilience-building.

Leadership.

Founder & CEO

Dr. Jackson Kingori

Neema Global Foundation is led by Dr. Jackson Kingori, Founder and CEO, together with Dr. Lucy Kingori, Co-Founder. They are internationally respected leaders recognized for their contributions to community development and public health management, respectively. Their leadership is driven by a shared passion for grassroots empowerment and a clear vision to nurture youth into ethical, innovative, and impactful leaders who contribute meaningfully to community transformation and national development.

NGF was founded in 2016 and formally incorporated in 2025, operating in partnership with Neema Gospel Church with programmatic operations in Kenya.
