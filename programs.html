Program Objectives and Expected Outcomes

Expand each objective to view its full outcomes.

Objective 1: Poverty Reduction & Decent Work

Promote sustainable livelihoods for widows, women, and youth through market-driven income opportunities, cooperative enterprises, and dignified self-employment.

Expected Outcomes

  • Increased household income and economic stability
  • Sustainable enterprises established and retained beyond 12 months
  • Improved savings behavior and reduced dependency on aid

Objective 2: Skills Development & Youth Leadership

Equip youth with market-relevant skills and ethical leadership competencies through partnerships with training institutions and industry professionals.

Expected Outcomes

  • Youth complete certified skills and leadership programs
  • Increased youth-led initiatives and civic participation
  • Strong mentorship retention and improved employability

Objective 3: Economic Empowerment & Climate-Smart Skills

Strengthen economic independence through vocational training, entrepreneurship support, financial literacy, and climate-smart technologies.

Expected Outcomes

  • Beneficiaries generate income through new skills
  • Improved business survival and revenue growth
  • Increased financial inclusion and literacy

Objective 4: Food Security & Agribusiness Development

Enhance food security and income resilience through climate-smart agriculture and agribusiness value-chain integration.

Expected Outcomes

  • Increased agricultural productivity and income
  • Improved access to structured markets
  • Reduced vulnerability to climate and market shocks

Objective 5: Community Empowerment & Social Justice

Strengthen social protection systems and advocate for policies that uphold the dignity, equity, and rights of widows and youth.

Expected Outcomes

  • Increased access to protection and legal support services
  • Greater representation in community leadership
  • Policy reforms influencing equity and inclusion

Objective 6: Climate Action & Environmental Resilience

Promote environmental conservation and community-led climate adaptation initiatives rooted in sustainable stewardship.

Expected Outcomes

  • Increased adoption of sustainable environmental practices
  • Improved ecosystem resilience and climate adaptation capacity
